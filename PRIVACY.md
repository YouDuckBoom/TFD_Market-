# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 2, 2026

## 1. Introduction
The "TFD Market Filter Saver" ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection, use, and disclosure practices.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**
- The Extension operates entirely locally within your browser.
- All filter presets and timestamps generated while using the Extension are stored locally on your device using standard browser storage APIs (`chrome.storage.local`).
- We do not track your browsing history, send your data to any external servers, or share it with third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The Extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics or tracking services. 

## 4. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository issues page.
